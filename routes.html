Mount Congreve Estate Gardens


From The Woodland Garden to The West Lawn, Mount Congreve is a stop worth visiting for it's stunning landscapes, floral extravagance. The Herman Dool Magnolia Walk oversees the River Suir and it's all framed by Magnolias of growth beginning as early as the 1960s.

In late summer a three quarter mile walk of over ninety different varieties of Hydrangeas is in full flower. As one enters the woodland garden there is a walk with over fifty varieties of Pieris, whose growth habits range from twenty inches to twenty feet, with new foliage emerging from pink to red...
